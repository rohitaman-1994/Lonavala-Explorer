Meet the Founder

I’m Rohit, the founder of Lonavala Explorer. Growing up in the Sahyadris, I discovered that travel here is not just about destinations but about moments—mist rising over valleys, chai shared at a hillside stall, or stories told beside ancient fort walls. With this vision, I built Lonavala Explorer to create trips that feel authentic, safe, and unhurried. Every itinerary reflects local insight, community connection, and care for the environment. My goal is simple: to help travelers experience Lonavala not as hurried tourists, but as welcomed guests discovering the heart of these hills. These hills shaped my idea of travel—unhurried, respectful, and curious.

We plan with weather windows, safe pacing, and small groups—a simple formula for remarkable days out.
