Lohagad Trek

Gentle ascent to a storied fort with broad Sahyadri panoramas. The Lohagad Trek is Lonavala’s most loved hike—easy, scenic, and steeped in history. The trail climbs steadily through mossy steps and stone gateways, reaching a fort top with sweeping Sahyadri views. In monsoon, mist and greenery make it magical; in winter, crisp skies reveal endless valleys. The highlight is the Vinchu Kata, a dramatic ridge spur shaped like a scorpion’s tail. With safe pacing and local stories, we make the trek accessible for beginners, families, and history buffs alike. It’s the perfect balance of gentle adventure, culture, and panoramic beauty in just a few hours.

Lake & Dam Drive

Relaxed loop with photogenic lakesides and reservoir viewpoints. The Lake & Dam Drive is Lonavala at its most relaxed. This scenic loop strings together reservoirs and lakes with carefully timed stops—still reflections in the morning, breezy dam views by midday, and golden sunsets at dusk. Roads are smooth, stops are easy to access, and pacing is tailored for families, photographers, or anyone seeking a calm day. Along the way, enjoy tea at local stalls and quiet lakeside moments. We travel respectfully, supporting communities and protecting nature. If you want wide horizons without strenuous treks, this drive is the most serene way to experience Lonavala.
